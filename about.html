About

I am a Ukrainian-English and Russian-English interpreter and translator working in the United Kingdom, across the EU, and internationally.

Work regularly involves judicial and arbitration contexts, international conferences, ministerial and parliamentary meetings, steering committees, government negotiations and technical consultations, policy discussions, implementation support missions, and bilateral dialogue across both public and private sectors - on-site, remotely, or in hybrid formats. Several relationships with institutional clients span a decade or more.

Academic background in translation and interpreting, international finance, and business administration complements this practical experience, particularly in assignments involving financial, regulatory, institutional, and commercial subject matter.

Professional standards, ethics, confidentiality, careful preparation, and consistency underpin all assignments, regardless of nature or scale.
