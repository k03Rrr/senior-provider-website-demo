Translation & Revision

Translation and Revision

I provide written translation, revision, editing, and proofreading services for institutional, legal, policy, financial, and technical materials, as well as for executive and private clients requiring accurate and discreet linguistic support.

Assignments typically involve documents that are relied upon in decision-making, submitted within formal procedures, or circulated among specialist audiences. In these contexts, accuracy extends beyond the transfer of meaning.

Terminology, register, consistency with existing materials, and awareness of the relevant legal, regulatory, or policy framework all contribute to the suitability of the final text for its intended purpose. Any client-specific requirements, style preferences, or house conventions are accommodated as part of this process.

Academic background in international finance, business administration, and translation and interpreting informs this work directly - particularly in financial, institutional, and legally sensitive materials where subject matter understanding is as important as linguistic precision.

Revision

Revision is carried out against the source text to verify accuracy, completeness, and consistency.

Editing

Editing addresses clarity, structure, style, and readability.

Proofreading

Proofreading provides a final review before submission or circulation.

Certified Translations

Certified translations can be provided where required for court, official, administrative, or regulatory purposes, in accordance with applicable professional and regulatory standards.

Working Process

Scope, deadlines, format requirements, and review stages are agreed at the outset. Where glossaries, reference materials, previous translations, or internal documentation are available, these are incorporated to ensure consistency and continuity across the wider body of work.

Confidentiality

All materials are treated with strict confidentiality. This applies as a matter of professional ethics across all contexts, and as a matter of obligation where documents form part of legal, regulatory, or formal proceedings.
